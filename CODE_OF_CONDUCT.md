# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

To foster an open and welcoming community, maintainers and contributors pledge to make participation in DEX a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and reviews.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical substance.
- Give clear, actionable feedback.
- Accept feedback gracefully.
- Help newcomers learn the project norms.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or exclusionary language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated bad-faith arguments that derail collaboration.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including Issues, Discussions, Pull Requests, and community channels linked from this repository.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues through:

1. A private maintainer contact request via Discussions, or
2. A direct maintainer issue request for confidential follow-up.

Please include context (links, screenshots, timestamps) where possible.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any appropriate action, including warning, temporary restrictions, or removal from community spaces for behavior that violates this policy.

Enforcement decisions are made to protect community safety and project health.
